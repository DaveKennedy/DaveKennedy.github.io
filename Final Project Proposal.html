Final Project Proposal — Assignment 7

Wooden Wallet with 3D Printed Inlay
 

 

Concept: 

For my final project I’ll use a combination of OnShape, Rhino, CNC Milling, and the 3D Printer to make a wooden wallet with rounded edges and an initial or logo inlay as a birthday present for a friend. To create this I would use OnShape to design all the sides of the wooden wallet so they will press fit together and eventually glued for permanence. To cut these shapes and create the space for the inlay I’ll use CNC Milling. Then I’ll use Rhino to design and create the inlay/logo that will printed on the 3D printer and later glued in to the inlay space on the wallet.
