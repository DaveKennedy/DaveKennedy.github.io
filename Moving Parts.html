How I did it:

I started by watching Joshua's tutorial video: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1WNmtTMGzSM517SliSAqm0r8SVZxWn9Mb Next I found inspiration from 507 Movements: http://507movements.com/mm_325.html I made all the guidelines in OnShape construction geometry. Then I started building the shapes. After several rounds of trying to get the dimensions right I added dimensions to it, made adjustments to the similar spaces and then exported a DWG file so I could print from Illustrator on to cardboard using the laser.

To make it move I glue the arms to a cut out of the circle on the two horizontal pieces shown above.
